Into the canopy

Hi — I'm Ian. I'm a software engineer based in Dallas, Texas, happiest when I'm turning a vague idea into something people can actually use. I care about clean systems, calm interfaces, and the small details that make software feel cared for.

Outside of my day job I tinker constantly: little tools, half-built games, and side projects like Notifly. If it can be prototyped on a weekend, I've probably tried.
