1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: your mobile phone number and the alert preferences or keywords you subscribe to.
  • Consent records: the date, time, and method of your opt-in and any opt-out, which we keep as a record of your consent.
  • Message activity: metadata about messages we send and your interactions with them (for example, delivery status and replies such as STOP or HELP), and whether your device supports RCS.
  • Website data: if you visit our website, standard log information such as your IP address and browser type may be collected by our hosting provider.

We do not collect payment card information through the Service. We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Information

  • to deliver the RCS/SMS alerts you have requested;
  • to manage your subscription, opt-in, and opt-out requests;
  • to operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Service;
  • to respond to your questions and provide support; and
  • to comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

3. Mobile Messaging & Opt-In Data

When you opt in, you consent to receive recurring automated RCS messages (with SMS fallback) from Claude Lego Alerts. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP to cancel at any time or HELP for help.

No sharing of mobile opt-in data for marketing. We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number, opt-in, or messaging consent with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information may be shared only with the service providers and subcontractors that help us operate the messaging program (for example, our messaging platform), and only as needed to deliver the Service. This consent and opt-in data is excluded from any other data sharing.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information only as follows:

  • Service providers: with vendors that perform services on our behalf, such as our messaging platform (for example, Twilio) and the RCS infrastructure operated by Google, solely to deliver and support the Service.
  • Legal and safety: when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to the commitments in this Policy.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their independent marketing.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you remain subscribed and as needed to provide the Service. After you opt out, we may retain a minimal record of your number and opt-out for as long as reasonably necessary to honor your request, maintain proof of consent, and comply with legal obligations.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • Opt out of messages: reply STOP to any message at any time.
  • Access and deletion: you may request access to, or deletion of, the personal information we hold about you by emailing lettersfromian@gmail.com.
  • Regional rights: depending on where you live (for example, California or the EEA/UK), you may have additional rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information. We will honor these rights as required by applicable law.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Third-Party Links

Our messages and website may link to third-party retailers or sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. International Users

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access it from outside the United States, you understand that your information may be processed in the United States, where data-protection laws may differ from those in your country.

11. Changes to This Policy
