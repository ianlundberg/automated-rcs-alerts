How to subscribe

Opt-in & consent

Notifly • Bricks sends opt-in alerts about LEGO® set launches, restocks, retiring sets, and price drops. Subscribing takes one text, and you can cancel anytime by replying STOP.

Text JOIN to +1 (000) 000-0000

Send the keyword JOIN from your phone to subscribe to Notifly • Bricks alerts. You'll get a confirmation message right back.

What you're signing up for

  • Program: Notifly • Bricks — recurring alerts on LEGO® launches, restocks, retiring sets & price drops.
  • How to opt in: Text JOIN to +1 (000) 000-0000.
  • Message frequency: Recurring; frequency varies based on the alerts you subscribe to.
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP at any time to cancel.
  • Help: Reply HELP, or email lettersfromian@gmail.com.
  • Consent is not a condition of any purchase.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
  • Your information is handled per our Privacy Policy, and use of the Service is subject to our Terms of Service.

How the opt-in works

  1. You text JOIN to +1 (000) 000-0000.
  2. We reply with a one-time welcome message confirming your subscription and the program details above.
  3. You start receiving Bricks alerts. Reply STOP anytime to unsubscribe, or HELP for help.
